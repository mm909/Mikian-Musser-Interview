Sunshine Nevada — Counselor

In 2015 I volunteered one week of my summer to be a counselor at a summer camp for kids with special needs. Little did I know, this one experience would change my life forever. I found an atmosphere of love and life-change at these camps and I could not stay away. Every year since then I have volunteered numerous weeks of my summer to give special kids an extra special summer.
